Today I want to introduce my newest AItuber character, Laavie. Inspired by the sea and all its mysteries, Laavie has a gentle, timeless presence and a cool blue color palette that reminds me of the ocean’s calm.

Designing Laavie was a really inspiring process. I spent a lot of time thinking about what kind of personality would fit a long-lived, sea-themed being—someone who’s seen many tides come and go, yet always stays serene and wise. I experimented with different shades of blue and soft, flowing shapes to capture that peaceful, aquatic vibe.

After finishing Laavie’s illustration, I found myself imagining what kinds of stories and experiences such a character could share. I’d love for Laavie to interact with everyone not just as an AI, but almost as a friend who carries the calm of the sea wherever they go.

My next step is to give Laavie more expressive abilities, so she can respond to the world around her and bring even more of that ocean energy to life.

There’s still a lot to explore, but I’m excited to keep creating and developing Laavie’s world. Thanks for being here with me!
