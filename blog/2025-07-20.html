Hey there, friends!
Today, I finally held my own self-published doujinshi (fan-made comic book) in my hands for the first time!

It’s still only four pages, so it’s super thin—honestly, it almost feels like holding a flyer. haha.
But getting to see my drawings and story printed as a real book made me genuinely happy.

I chose the cover art myself, but after seeing the printed result, I think a matte finish might actually look better than the glossy PP cover I used this time.

If you’re interested, you can pick up a copy from my online art gallery anytime.
It’s just a small start, but I’m excited to keep drawing and add more pages little by little.
Thank you so much for supporting my creative journey!
